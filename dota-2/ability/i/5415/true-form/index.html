True Form
No Target
Lone Druid learns to morph himself into a raging bear, losing his ranged advantage but gaining melee power as well as the Battle Cry ability. He can morph freely between druid and bear form.
 
BASE ATTACK TIME: 1.5
BONUS ARMOR: 4 / 6 / 8
BONUS HP: 250 / 400 / 600
SPEED LOSS: 35
