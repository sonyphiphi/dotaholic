Sven Guide

At start u must choose between [item=182] and [item=11]. If u gonna try kill on lane or get heavily harrased buy [item=182]. If u cant kill line enemy or get freefarm [item=11] helps get lasthits and farm faster later.
Sven feel good on easy line, but can go to hard line with strong support. Feel free to gank mid if u cant farm well on line or can get good rune.
Buy fast [item=6] if u need regen badly and suffer too much harras. When u get fast [item=63][item=151] with ult on u can kill almost anyone, even 1vs2, 1vs3 if u can effectivly activate\deactivate armlet to get free ~500 hp.
Then u must define what u get first [item=172] or [item=116]. If u cant even hit 2-3 times before diening in 5vs5 fight -> go [item=116], else get [item=172] for SUPERB DAMAGE. For now u can kill almost any heroes in 1-2 sec with your ult.
If u get yours GREAT THREE ITEMS ([item=151][item=172][item=116]) before 30-35 min then you can just rape enemy team, and push very fast.
